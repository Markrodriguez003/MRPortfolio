About me

I've always been an artist. I enjoy the process of creating something out of nothing, the frustration of design and chasing the high of finishing something. My first taste of the arts is music . I am a musician with multiple projects running side by side of each other. I am hopefully going to release some of my music soon. I have my hands in too many cookie jars at the moment...

I decided to branch out and pursue different fields of art and I stumbled across web development. The designing, editing and implementation of websites and applications really appealed to me. I decided to take it and run as far as I can with it. I had a really good friend inspire me to take up on programming and wonderful support from my family. So far I have tackled C++, python Java, and the typical full stack: HTML, CSS & Javascript. My thirst never ends! I am planning on releasing some of my projects soon, soo please check back to my page for the latest updates!

I have been a musician from an very early age. I was tapping on imaginary drums and humming made up melodies like a carnary since I was a wee lad. It is in my blood it seems and honestly music really made me the person that I am today. It was my first area of exploration in the world of arts. My first instrument I picked up was the guitar. Now I am experienced with a multitude of instruments such as bass, synthesizers, mandolins and even vocals!

If you asked me what type of genre of music I listen to, I would tell you pretty much everything.
